Services

________SOIL TESTING _________

The scope includes collection of soil & rock samples from the field and carrying out the required laboratory & other tests to assess the safe bearing capacity of soil in order to facilitate safe & precautionary measure at the stage of designing. The investigation is presented in the form of a report containing all the findings of the test. All the laboratory tests are conducted by specialists & authenticity is certified by Government Testing Laboratory

________ARCHITECTURAL DESIGN _________

The scope includes pre-design study, site evaluation with existing features (both physical & socio-cultural), climatic analysis, preparation of feasibility report and proposing conceptual design. The revision of the preliminary conceptual design is done after discussion with the client. Once the design is finalized, approval plan is prepared for getting NOC from the local development authorities and municipal corporations. Once the approval is obtained, the final working drawing is prepared and the same is released to structural Engineer for consultancy. Once the structural clearance is obtained, further working details are prepared and released for execution with active supervision to ensure every detail is implemented as per original visualization.

________ INTERIOR DESIGN _________

The scope include study of existing civil buildup space and formulation of the design brief or concept as per the user requirement, layout of furniture including detailing of some customized furniture, detailing of illumination system, air conditioning, specification of materials & other technical details with detailed project execution schedule.

________STRUCTURAL DESIGN _________

This includes the scope of analyzing the soil testing reports, calculating and preparing structural design according to the same and providing adequate drawings to carry out the work. The design is done keeping in mind the use of best available technology (latest version of STAAD PRO) and the locally available material, giving special emphasis on the low cost building technology.

________ PLUMBING SERVICES _________

This includes the scope of planning, designing & preparing the layout plan and network design of plumbing services for residential, commercial, industrial projects. The pipes used in the supply of water to be harmless to human beings & environmental friendly.

____ELECTRICAL & NETWORK DESIGN ____

This includes the scope of planning electrical services and preparing the network design for residential, commercial, industrial projects.
